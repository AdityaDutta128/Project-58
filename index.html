My Favorite Hobby:
Reading Books

Reading is a very addictive and interesting hobby and I am absorbed in it. I love reading diffrent types of genres such as fantasy, adventure, science-fiction, mystery, etc. My favorite one is adventure. The authors who have mesmerized me to read more and more of their books are David Walliams and R.J PALACIO. Reading helps us in many ways such as it improves our grammer, creativity, vocabulary, etc. But I think that reading mainly encourages us to think out of the box.
